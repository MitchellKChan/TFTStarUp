⭐️ Welcome to TFT Star Up! ⭐️


TFT Star Up is a tool for practicing one's rerolling skills based on gameplay from the auto battler videogame Teamfight Tactics (TFT). Players may need to refresh their shop multiple times (“rolling down”) within a single planning phase to find particular units they want, and how well they can repeatedly refresh and buy units within a short time can often be the difference between victory and defeat.


Controls


The tool focuses exclusively on the game aspects integral to rolling down: the Shop and Bench.

The Shop section at the bottom of the screen contains the 5 units you are offered at a time, which can be rerolled by either pressing the "D" key or clicking on the "Refresh" button. Shop units will display their name, image, and tier denoted by color (gray is the lowest, gold is the highest). Units have certain odds of appearing in the shop based on their tier; as your level increased, odds for higher tiers increase and odds for lower tiers decrease. Units can be purchased by clicking on them, which will appear on the bench.


The Bench section above the Shop can hold up to 9 units, which appear there when bought from the shop. When a third copy of a unit on the bench is purchased, the three copies combine to "star up" that unit to a 2-star copy. Units can be sold to free up bench space by hovering over them and pressing the "E" key.


You may adjust certain settings for the rolldown before beginning. Please enjoy!
